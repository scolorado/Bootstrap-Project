Welcome to Patriot Nation!

Our teams and clubs have won many awards, including several State Championships, in our school’s storied history. Freehold Township High School is a member of the New Jersey State Interscholastic Athletic Association (NJSIAA) and the Class A - North of the Shore Conference.

Requirements

All athletes must submit required forms each season, according to the process detailed below.

A valid Pre-Participation Physical Evaluation Packet dated within one year of the season’s start date, that includes a physical evaluation completed by a licensed physician. To determine if the date of your last physical is valid:

  • Review the sports physical exam date found on the “Summary” tab in the Genesis Parent Portal
  • Students can check with the Health Office

If a valid physical IS on file in the health office (within one year of the season’s start date), you do not need to complete the Preparticipation Physical Evaluation Packet for this season. If a valid physical is NOT on file in the health office (NOT within one year of the season’s start date):

  • The Pre-Participation Physical Evaluation Packet is required for this season
  • Print and complete ALL 4 PAGES with the same date, and submit by the deadlines to the Health or Main Office

The following forms must be completed online in the Genesis Parent Portal; accessed in the “Forms” tab:

  1. Activity / Athletic Eligibility Form: Must be completed EACH SEASON by the submission deadline for students participating in multiple sports.
  2. Health History Update Form: Must be completed EACH SEASON by the submission deadline for students participating in multiple sports.
  3. Athletic Emergency Information:Must be completed once each academic year.
  4. Sports-Related Concussion and Head Injury Fact Sheet: Must be completed once each academic year
  5. The Sudden Cardiac Death in Young Athletes Information Sheet:Must be completed once each academic year.
  6. NJSIAA Steroid Testing Policy: Must be completed once each academic year.
  7. Use and Misuse of Opioid Drugs: Must be completed once each academic year AND Athletes vs. Opioids video must be viewed each season.

Option 2


The Freehold Regional High School District encourages all students to participate fully in the physical education program. It recognizes, however, that some students are engaged in athletic, interscholastic, or other programs of vigorous physical activity, allowing for achievement of the New Jersey Core Curriculum Content Standards. Such students may, pursuant to rules of the State Board of Education, be excused from the required enrollment in a physical education course, while still earning physical education credit. There are three qualifying paths:

  • Path A (for 10th through 12th graders): Participation in two or more NJSIAA-recognized JV and/or Varsity sports or two seasons of JV/Varsity cheerleading at the high school
  • Path B (for 10th through 12th graders): Participation in one NJSIAA-recognized JV or Varsity sport or JV/Varsity cheerleading at the high school, plus documented off-season-training (150 minutes/week) in the same sport/cheer with an organization approved by the principal
  • Path C (for 9th through 12th graders): Continuous high-level training, practice, performance, and/or competition in an athletic or physical activity for at least 150 minutes per week with an approved organization, coach, and/or teacher.

The Option II activities must provide at least 150 minutes of physical education weekly. Students must have the coach or instructor verify this via a weekly log. Any parent who wishes to exercise the Option II Alternative Physical Education program for a child must submit an online Option II Application. All students who participate are enrolled in a Study Hall and will receive an “S” (Satisfactory) upon successful completion of Option II.
